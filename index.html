About the Robbins-Madanes Training School

Robbins-Madanes Training is the official coaching and certification school of Tony Robbins and Cloe Madanes. Since being founded in 2009, we have trained and certified more than 15,000 life coaches around the world. Our mission is to foster a movement of dedicated, compassionate, and professional agents of change who can follow in the footsteps of Tony and Cloe to help make the world a better place for everyone.

Meet Your Principal Teachers

Tony Robbins

Entrepreneur & Life Strategist

Tony Robbins is an entrepreneur, #1 New York Times best-selling author, philanthropist, the nation’s #1 life & business strategist, and a pioneer of the coaching industry. For more than four and a half decades, millions of people have enjoyed the warmth, humor, and transformational power of Tony’s business and personal development events.

Cloe Madanes

World-Renowned Psychotherapist

Cloe was a direct student of intervention master Milton Erickson and was one of the originators of the strategic approach. She is one of the world’s most respected authorities in the field of psychotherapy and coaching. Her ten books are considered classics in the profession and have been translated into 30 languages. Cloe’s innovations are taught in universities all over the world.

The Program Curriculum

"Your training with Robbins-Madanes isn’t just a collection of courses: it’s a complete roadmap for mastering Tony Robbins’ proven coaching methodologies, guided step-by-step by Cloe Madanes."

Core 100: Foundations of Strategic Coaching

(100 hours)

Your starting point for mastering Tony Robbins’ most impactful coaching strategies, broken down by Cloe Madanes. You’ll learn how to create breakthroughs for individuals in any life stage, resolve inner conflicts, and apply powerful intervention skills in real time. Outcome: Confidence in applying proven coaching tools with clients, colleagues, and loved ones.

Core 200: Advanced Coaching Mastery

(100 hours)

Takes you deeper into relationship and family dynamics, advanced negotiation, group coaching, and resilience work. You’ll study Tony’s most challenging interventions (narrated and explained by Cloe), and practice applying them with precision. Outcome: Ability to handle complex coaching scenarios, from marital issues to group conflict.

Total Breakthrough Training

(22 weeks)

Specialized training in family and relationship coaching. Learn to address patterns across generations, navigate crises, and create lasting change within family units. Outcome: Skills to help clients rebuild and strengthen their closest relationships.

Executive Coaching 100 & 200

(100 hours each)

Learn how to coach high-level leaders on performance, decision-making, and work-life balance. Includes opportunities to practice with real executive clients. Outcome: Confidence to work with CEOs, executives, and leadership teams.

Working With Women

(25 hours)

Focused on the unique challenges and strengths of women in personal and professional life. You’ll learn how to help female clients access their strengths, overcome barriers, and thrive. Outcome: Expanded expertise in serving women clients effectively.

NLP Mastery Program

(duration varies)

Master advanced language and behavior-change tools, including sensory acuity, submodalities, anchoring, and meta-programs. Outcome: Greater flexibility and precision in influencing positive change.

Marketing for Coaches

Step-by-step business-building system designed for coaches. Covers client attraction, offer creation, and sustainable growth strategies. Outcome: Ability to consistently attract and retain coaching clients.

6 Human Needs

(self-paced)

Learn Tony Robbins’ core model for understanding human motivation and behavior, and how to use it to create rapid connection and lasting change. Outcome: Deeper insight into what drives people, and how to help them fulfill those needs in healthy ways.

Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does this program work? Will I have the time?

Each program is a hybrid learning experience designed for flexibility. You'll engage with self-paced online modules, attend live coaching calls with Cloe and the faculty, and participate in interactive practice sessions. This structure allows you to fit the training around your current work and life commitments. Many of our students are professionals, entrepreneurs, and parents who successfully complete the program while maintaining a busy schedule.

What is expected of me when I join?

We ask that you bring your full commitment to the process. To get the most out of the training, you should be ready to engage with the video lessons, actively participate in live sessions, and complete the practice activities. Our faculty and community are here to support you every step of the way, so your primary role is to show up, be open to learning, and apply the tools you're given.

Do I need prior coaching experience?

Not at all. Our programs are designed to be accessible and impactful for everyone, from individuals with no coaching experience to seasoned professionals in other fields (like doctors, lawyers, and teachers) who want to add powerful new skills to their toolkit. The curriculum starts with the foundations and builds progressively, ensuring you're ready for every step of the journey.

Is the program accredited?

Yes. Robbins-Madanes Training is accredited by the International Coaching Federation (ICF). Our programs meet and exceed the rigorous standards for coach training and certification, ensuring that your training and certification are recognized globally.

How much does the program cost?

The total investment for our programs varies depending on which curriculum path you choose. During your free discovery call, an Admissions Advisor will work with you to understand your goals and provide a clear breakdown of the costs, available payment plans, and any exclusive offers.

What if I’m not sure which program is right for me?

That’s exactly what your free discovery call is for. Our Admissions Advisors are trained to listen to your unique goals and professional background. They will provide a personalized recommendation on the best program to start with, ensuring you have the right roadmap for your success.
